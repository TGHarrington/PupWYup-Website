The service we provide.

Puppies with Yuppies is a dog breeding optimization research destination.
The attributes and personality types that are desirable in your ideal dog can be researched.
The breeds they are attributed to can be identified.
Those breeds can then be combined with breeds containing other desirable attributes.
Feel free to experiment with differing genetic combinations to design your ideal canine.
